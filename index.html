OFFICE OF THE DEAN OF STUDENTS

Welcome to the Office of the Dean of Students at the University of Maryland Eastern Shore (UMES)! We are here to support you on your academic journey and personal growth. At UMES, we pride ourselves on fostering a vibrant, inclusive, and supportive community where every student has the opportunity to thrive.

ABOUT THE DEAN OF STUDENTS OFFICE

The Dean of Students Office is dedicated to enhancing the student experience by advocating for your needs, providing resources, and promoting a culture of respect and accountability. Our mission is to empower students to succeed both in and out of the classroom while upholding the core values of UMES.

SERVICES WE OFFER

We are committed to assisting you in various areas to ensure a holistic and fulfilling university experience. Our services include:

Student Advocacy

Support for students navigating challenges and ensuring their voices are heard.

Conflict Resolution

Assistance with resolving disputes and fostering harmonious relationships.

Counseling Referrals

Guidance to mental health resources and support services.

Campus Engagement

Opportunities to get involved in student organizations and leadership development programs.

Emergency Support

Immediate assistance during crises, including housing or financial hardships.

OUR COMMITMENT TO YOU

At the University of Maryland Eastern Shore, we believe every student deserves a safe, supportive, and enriching college experience. The Dean of Students Office is your partner in navigating university life. Together, we will help you achieve your goals and make your time at UMES unforgettable.

We look forward to working with you and wish you an amazing experience here at UMES!
